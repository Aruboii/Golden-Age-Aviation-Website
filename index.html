Welcome to Golden Age Aviation

Disclaimer,

Golden Age Aviation is a virtual airline for home flight-simulation enthusiasts. It is not a real-world airline, does not operate aircraft, and does not sell or book flights.

Golden Age Aviation is a non-commercial hobby community that simulates historical airline operations across multiple carriers, including (but not limited to) Lufthansa (DLH), Air India (AIC), Kingfisher (KFR), Jet Airways (JAI), and Singapore Airlines (SIA). Our aim is to recreate schedules, fleets, and procedures from the “golden age of aviation” as closely as reasonably possible for educational and entertainment purposes.

We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Lufthansa, Air India, Kingfisher Airlines, Jet Airways, Singapore Airlines, or any of their parent companies, subsidiaries, or partners. All trademarks, service marks, logos, and liveries remain the property of their respective owners. Any real-world references (airline names, aircraft types, routes, logos, imagery) are used for historical simulation and identification only. If a rights holder requests modification or removal of material, we will act promptly.

No commercial activity is conducted via this website/community. Membership is voluntary; participation requires a consumer flight-simulator platform and add-ons chosen by each member. All flight activity is virtual.

For the official airlines, please visit their real-world websites:

• Kingfisher Airlines :- (defunct; historical reference only)
