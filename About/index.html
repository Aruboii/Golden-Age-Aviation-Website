About Us

Step back in time with Golden Age Aviation — an airline dedicated to bringing the spirit of aviation’s most iconic eras back to life. Our mission is simple: to provide pilots with the unique opportunity to fly and experience historic aircraft and legendary routes once operated by the great airlines of the past.

It’s the elegant days of propliners, the dawn of the jet age, or the golden years of aviation innovation, we recreate the experience through carefully designed schedules, retro liveries, and classic aircraft operations. At Golden Age Aviation, we aren’t just flying planes — we’re reliving history, keeping the stories of aviation’s past alive in the skies of today.

Our Airlines

Kingfisher Airlines

Kingfisher Airlines represents premium comfort and style in the skies. Known for its luxury service and attention to detail, it brings back the charm of flying with elegance and care.

Jet Airways

Jet Airways embodies India’s modern aviation spirit, connecting passengers worldwide with efficiency and warmth. With a legacy of professionalism and service, it remains a symbol of reliability and trust in the skies.

Singapore Airlines

Singapore Airlines is a global benchmark for quality and hospitality. Celebrated for exceptional service and innovative standards, it continues to redefine the experience of long-haul travel.

Lufthansa

Lufthansa stands as Europe’s aviation powerhouse, blending tradition with cutting-edge innovation. Its global reach, strong network, and commitment to safety make it one of the most respected names in aviation.

Air India

Air India carries the legacy of India’s flag carrier, combining heritage with service to connect people across continents. With its distinctive Maharaja identity, it reflects pride, tradition, and a welcoming spirit.
